§ 01 — Origin
A new category
Autonomy, made desirable.
We wrote the future.
Then we brought it here.
Abbiamo scritto il futuro.
Poi lo abbiamo portato qui.

SWARM is a future consumer technology brand built on autonomous, distributed intelligence. Spiritual successor of Dormio: same skeleton, ring above tracked-out capitals, absolute black.

Many units. One intention. The brand voice mirrors the product — coordinated and quiet. SWARM does not justify itself, does not bounce, does not whoosh. It arrives.

Color appears only when something activates. Type whispers. Negative space is a feature. The ring is the signature.

Rule 01

Black is the canvas.

A SWARM page is more black than content. Never a white default. Never a gradient on chrome.

Rule 02

Accents are state.

Blue, green, amber are not decoration. If it isn't a status, it isn't allowed to use them.

Rule 03

Borders, not shadows.

1px Gunmetal hairlines + 1px inset platinum highlight on the top edge. Machined, not soft.

§ 05 — Voice
SWARM speaks rarely
When it does, it leaves a mark.
How we sound
Short. Sentences are sculpted. A claim is rarely more than seven words.
Visionary, not hyped. The future is stated, not sold.
Third-person and imperative. Rarely we, almost never you.
Periods are weapons. Em-dashes for pivots.
Bilingual cadence — English and Italian, matching weight.
Exclamation marks. Emoji. Marketing semicolons.

Construction
  • Grid24×24 px square; visual content fills 20px. The ring lives on the 20px circle whenever possible.
  • Stroke1.5px, round caps, round joins. No flourishes, no perspective, no skeumorphism.
  • FillStroke-only. The only exception is a small filled dot for "signal", "node count", or "unit ID".
  • ColorPlatinum at rest. Muted Silver when secondary. State color only when the icon represents that state.
  • FamilyCustom set first. Lucide as the open-source fallback — same stroke language.
  • EmojiNever. Unicode allowed only when it carries the geometry: ○ • — ↗.
§ 07 — Motion
Slow. Precise. Settled.
SWARM does not bounce.
Loader · 900ms
A single revolution.

One ring tracing once, slowly. Never a spinner. Glacial easing.

Breath · 4000ms
The ring breathes.

Opacity 0.6 → 1.0 → 0.6 over 4 seconds. The only ambient animation.

Connect · 2400ms
State pulses once.

When something activates, a single soft halo expands. Then silence.

Easing
cubic-bezier(0.2, 0.7, 0.1, 1)
A long, settled deceleration. The Swarm easing.
Hover
Brightness shift only.
filter: brightness(1.1). Never a color change.
Press
Scale to 0.98 · 120ms.
No color flash, no bounce-back.
Focus
A 1px Orbital Blue ring.
The only allowed color — focus is a state.
